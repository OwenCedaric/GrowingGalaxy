The web has become loud. Ceda.is is an attempt to turn down the volume.

## Immersive Reading

By removing sidebars, pop-ups, and cluttered navigation, we allow the reader to descend into the topic. This is the concept of the "Digital Lounge"—a place where time slows down.

### Key Elements of the Lounge

- **Soft Transitions:** Avoid jarring page loads.
- **Micro-animations:** Subtle movements that guide the eye.
- **Matte Aesthetics:** High contrast but low saturation to reduce eye strain.

We believe that the future of content curation is not in more volume, but in more depth.
