What happens when we remove the visual anchor of an image? We are forced to confront the pure form of the word.

## The Power of Typography

In the absence of a hero image, typography must carry the emotional weight of the content. The serif choice, the tracking, and the generous negative space become the "image" themselves.

### The Void

The void is not empty; it is a space waiting to be filled by the reader's imagination. By leaving out the literal representation (the photo), we invite a more personal interpretation of the text.
